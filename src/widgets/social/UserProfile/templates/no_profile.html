What is a KBase Public Profile?

A KBase profile is simply a set of facts about that you that you establish about yourself, including a photo or avatar, your affiliations, the type of work you do, and a personal statement. Most of these fields are optional. Only you can change your profile--no other user can edit it. Changes you make to your profile are instantly active.

Who can see your profile?

Your profile is is visible to all KBase members and staff through the KBase web applications, and the public at large through the standard public profile viewer page. It is not indexed by Google or other search engines.

Why have a KBase profile?

Having a profile makes it easier for other users to become familiar with you and your work, which facilitates the global knowledge-sharing vision of KBase.
